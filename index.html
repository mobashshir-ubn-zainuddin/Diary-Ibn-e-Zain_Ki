Diary Ibn-e-Zain Ki

Ghazal

Barish Aur Shayari

Ae abr-e-khizan yaha barsa na kar
Mere purane zakhmo ko kureda na kar
Badal gyi hai ab kaifiyat meri bahut
Wo purane lamhon ko phir taza na kar
Teri aahat mujh ko mahsoor kar degi
Ye ada teri mujh ko khuda se dur kar degi
Hue arson jo mila tha mai uske husn se
Uski ki yaad mujh ko phir beqarar kar degi

Mukalma

Jab usse maine apna nata tod liya
Uske ghar ka chakkar lagana chhod diya
Uski gali me bhatakna to door ki baat hai
Uski gali ki taraf nazar karna chhod diya
Khat hua aaj uska mujh ko mausool ki ab
Mujh se tumne kyu muh apna mod liya
Kal tak jo bhatakte the dar par didar ko
Ab to galiyon me bhi aana chhod diya
Mai aaj kal bahut bimar si rehti hun
Tumne to meri ayadat karna chhod diya
Wafa kiya tha maine bas izhar baaqi tha
Tumne to ishq ka jam pilana chhod diya

Nazm

Ek Aasi Ki Dua

Bhawar mein gunaahon ke hu mai fasa ae Khuda
Sattar-o-Ghaffar-o-Hajat rawa mushkil kusha ae Khuda
Gunahon ke daldal me dhakela mujh ko nafs ki ghulami ne
Hai mujh se doobton ka tu hi sahaara ae Khuda
Nafs-o-Shetaan ke chaalon se naamahram tha main
Gunaahon mein bohat hi khush-o-khurram tha main
Hai yeh teri hi laa-inteha rehmat ka zahoor
In sab ke baad bhi teri nazar mein mukarram tha main
Hai tujh ko Fatubu-iIallahi-Mataaba ka waasta
Hai tu sattar maaon se bhi meherbaan zyada
Kar mujh par nazr-e-karam,ho jaon mai tujh se qarib
Keh tujh ko mahboob la-mahdood hai yeh ada
Baad iske keh ho mujh ko ataa woh noor-e-imaani
Keh na ho jisme gunaahon ko karne mein aasani
Bana de mujh ko aisa de kar dhaal taqwa ka
Keh na ho anjaam mera aakhirat me shaytaani

College Me Behayayi

Nigaah ko jhukaau to hadsat,uthaau to waardaat
Kashmakash mein hu mai ae sahab-e-kash hazrat
Aam hai uryaniyat aur behijabi yahan
Nazar ko mushkil se bhi panah milti kahan
Apne tahzeeb-o-tamaddun ke tareeqat ko bhula baithe
Maghrib ke zehar ko mashriq ke fizaon mein ghula baithe
Hai yahan auraton ke libas mardon se tangtar aur kamtar
Hain mard o zan yahan ke nahin kuchh Chicago London se behtar
Haya ka janaza uthakar hi dam lenge yeh me'amar-e-qaum
Bachna ho jo in ke fitne se Ibn-e-Zain to rakhiye soum
Astaghfirullah ka wird subah-o-shaam kya kijiye
Ihdinas-siraat-al-mustaqeem ki Rab se dua kijiye

Rubayi

Inkar

Mayil hua mai jo uski taraf to socha izhar ho jae
Izhar-e-Mohabbat kya yaaron ab zara iqrar ho jae
Darta tha mai ki jawab me kahin inkar na mile
Ye soch kar sochta hu ki ye soch ab farar ho jae

Talash

Hai zindagi me gham bahut,madawa chahiye
Hu mai sukhanwar achchha,hamnawa chahiye
Logon ne zakhm diye hai bahut zyada bas
Is takleef ko kam karne ke liye dawa chahiye

BeTa'alluqui

Kaise kahe ki tujhko bhi hamse hai wasta koi
Ham ne to kabhi apni manzil ko ek jana nhi
Yu lagta hai tu hai kisi aur hi gali ka hai musafir
Ibn-e-Zain ne kabhi tujhe apne rahguzar me paya nhi
